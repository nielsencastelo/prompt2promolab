
# Exemplos de prompts — NazaApp

## Prompt base para vídeo curto
A church secretary using a modern web dashboard and a smartphone with the NazaApp mobile app, smooth camera movement, bright and organized church office, warm natural light, realistic promotional style, trustworthy and modern atmosphere, show productivity and connection with the church community

## Prompt base para CTA final
A pastor and team smiling after organizing church activities with a mobile app and web platform, subtle cinematic camera push-in, church lobby at sunset, warm golden light, hopeful and uplifting mood, end frame ready for call to action

## Negative prompt sugerido
blurry, distorted hands, broken phone screen, unreadable interface, bad text, duplicated people, warped anatomy, unstable motion, flickering, low detail

## Estrutura recomendada
- cena 1: problema atual
- cena 2: organização da rotina
- cena 3: comunicação com membros
- cena 4: agenda e ministérios
- cena 5: financeiro ou relatórios
- cena 6: CTA
